Soka Senbei

A traditional flavor that dates back to the Edo period. A representative delicacy of Saitama with a savory soy sauce aroma and rich rice flavor that fills your mouth.

Average Price: Around 150 yen (tax included)

Storage Instructions: Avoid high temperature and humidity. Consume as soon as possible after opening.

Shelf Life: About 2 months
