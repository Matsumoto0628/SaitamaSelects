Gokabo

A traditional Japanese sweet made from glutinous rice and generously coated with roasted soybean flour (kinako). It offers a gentle and nostalgic flavor.

Average Price: Around 180 yen (tax included)

Storage Instructions: Store at room temperature, avoiding direct sunlight and high humidity.

Shelf Life: About 30 days
