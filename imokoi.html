Imokoi

A heartwarming traditional sweet made with carefully selected sweet potatoes and mildly sweet red bean paste wrapped in rice flour dough.

Average Price: Around 200 yen (tax included)

Storage Instructions: Store at room temperature, avoiding direct sunlight, high temperatures, and humidity

Shelf Life: Approximately 5 days
