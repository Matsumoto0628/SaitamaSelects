Three Famous Sweets of Saitama

Perfect as souvenirs or as a treat for yourself.

Imokoi

A nostalgic Japanese sweet made by gently wrapping sweet potatoes and red bean paste in rice flour dough.
Soka Senbei

A crispy and savory rice cracker, a specialty of Saitama. The soy sauce flavor is simply irresistible.
Gokabo

A traditional sweet from Saitama, known for its roasted soybean flour aroma and gentle sweetness.
